Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CarbonChecks

General Questions

What is CarbonChecks and how does it work?

CarbonChecks is a free online carbon footprint calculator that helps you understand your environmental impact. Simply enter information about your lifestyle (transportation, energy usage, diet, etc.), and our calculator provides an estimate of your annual CO2 emissions along with personalized tips to reduce your impact.

Is the carbon footprint calculator free to use?

Yes, our carbon footprint calculator is completely free to use. We believe that environmental awareness should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial situation. We support our free service through advertising and affiliate partnerships with eco-friendly companies.

How accurate are the carbon footprint calculations?

Our calculator uses industry-standard emission factors and provides estimates based on your inputs. While it's not a scientific measurement, it gives you a good understanding of your environmental impact and areas for improvement. The calculations are based on peer-reviewed research and government data sources.

Calculator Questions

What information do I need to calculate my carbon footprint?

You'll need to provide information about:

  • Weekly car miles driven
  • Number of flights taken per year (short and long haul)
  • Meat consumption frequency
  • Monthly electricity usage (kWh)
  • Household size

Don't worry if you don't know exact numbers - estimates are fine and will still give you valuable insights.

How do I find my monthly electricity usage?

You can find this information on your electricity bill. Look for "kWh" (kilowatt-hours) used in the billing period. If you don't have access to your bill, you can estimate based on your home size and appliances. The average US household uses about 900 kWh per month.

What if I don't know my exact numbers?

That's perfectly fine! Our calculator is designed to work with estimates. Even rough estimates will give you valuable insights into your carbon footprint and areas for improvement. You can always recalculate with more accurate numbers later.

Privacy and Data

Is my personal information safe and private?

Yes, we take your privacy seriously. All calculations are performed locally in your browser, so your personal data never leaves your device unless you explicitly provide it (like subscribing to our newsletter). We don't store your carbon footprint calculations or personal information.

Do you sell my data to third parties?

No, we never sell your personal data to third parties. We only collect minimal information (like email addresses for our newsletter) with your explicit consent, and we use this information only to provide our services to you.

Reducing Your Carbon Footprint

What are the most effective ways to reduce my carbon footprint?

The most effective actions depend on your current lifestyle, but generally include:

  • Switching to renewable energy sources
  • Reducing meat consumption
  • Using public transportation or electric vehicles
  • Improving home energy efficiency
  • Flying less frequently

Our calculator provides personalized recommendations based on your specific situation.

How often should I recalculate my carbon footprint?

We recommend recalculating your carbon footprint every 3-6 months, or whenever you make significant lifestyle changes such as moving to a new home, changing jobs, adopting new sustainable habits, or purchasing a new vehicle.

Are the products and services you recommend legitimate?

Yes, we carefully vet all recommended products and services. We only partner with companies that have proven track records in sustainability and environmental responsibility. However, we always recommend doing your own research before making significant purchases.

Technical Issues

The calculator isn't working on my device. What should I do?

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  • Refresh the page and try again
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Make sure JavaScript is enabled in your browser
  • Try using a different browser
  • Check if you have any ad blockers that might be interfering

If the problem persists, please contact our support team.

Can I use the calculator on my mobile device?

Yes, our calculator is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones and tablets. The interface automatically adjusts to your screen size for the best user experience.
