Frequently Asked Questions

Before reaching out, you might find the answer to your question in our FAQ section:

How accurate is the carbon footprint calculator?

Our calculator uses industry-standard emission factors and provides estimates based on your inputs. While it's not a scientific measurement, it gives you a good understanding of your environmental impact and areas for improvement.

Is my data secure and private?

Yes, we take your privacy seriously. All calculations are performed locally in your browser, and we don't store your personal information unless you explicitly provide it (like subscribing to our newsletter).

Do you charge for using the calculator?

No, our carbon footprint calculator is completely free to use. We believe that environmental awareness should be accessible to everyone.

How often should I recalculate my carbon footprint?

We recommend recalculating your carbon footprint every 3-6 months, or whenever you make significant lifestyle changes (like moving, changing jobs, or adopting new sustainable habits).

Can I share my results on social media?

Absolutely! We encourage you to share your carbon footprint results and sustainability journey. Use the hashtag #CarbonChecks to connect with our community.
